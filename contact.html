What To Include

Your first message does not need to be perfect. If you include the basics below, I can guide the rest.

  • Business type: what you sell or what service you provide
  • Website: where you want traffic to land
  • Current Pinterest: link to your account (if you have one)
  • Goal: what “working” would look like for you

Quick Answers

What happens after I email you?

I’ll reply with a few questions to understand your goals and current setup. If it looks like a fit, we’ll schedule a short call to confirm scope and next steps.

Do I need to be posting on Pinterest already?

Not necessarily. Some clients come with an existing account that needs structure and optimization. Others start from a clean foundation. Either way, the process is the same: keywords, structure, implementation, refinement.

Is this ongoing management?

PinTrends is strategy-led implementation. The goal is to build a system you can maintain. If you want ongoing support after the five-week implementation, we can discuss what makes sense.
